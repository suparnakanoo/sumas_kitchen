Namastey! My name is Suma Kanoo, and I have a deep love for cooking, I love to cook for my family and friends. To be honest, starting my channel wasn't easy. I had no professional equipment, and my kitchen was far from fancy. But I didn't let that discourage me. With a simple camera and lots of enthusiasm, I began filming my cooking adventures right there in my cozy kitchen. My channel isn't what you would call "super popular." I don't have millions of subscribers or viral videos. But you know what? That's perfectly fine with me. I'm not here for the numbers; I'm here because I genuinely enjoy sharing my recipes and connecting with like-minded food enthusiasts. On my channel, you'll find a collection of my favorite recipes, from comforting family meals to delightful desserts. Sure, I may stumble over my words or drop a spoon from time to time, but that's what makes it real and relatable, right? As a housewife, balancing my responsibilities at home with my YouTube journey has been challenging. But I'm determined to make it work. And you know what? Despite the small size of my channel, the support and love I receive from my viewers is overwhelming. Every comment, like, and share means the world to me. It's the encouragement I need to keep going, to keep sharing my culinary creations with passion and authenticity. Sure, I haven't landed any big collaborations or published a cookbook yet, but that doesn't define my success. To me, success is measured by the joy I bring to others through my cooking, the connections I make with my viewers, and the personal growth I experience along the way. So, if you're looking for simple, heartfelt recipes and a friendly face to share them with, I invite you to join me on my humble YouTube journey. Together, let's celebrate the joy of cooking, one delicious recipe at a time.
